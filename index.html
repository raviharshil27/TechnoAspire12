Welcome! This is TechnoAspire'12, a technical cum cultural festival at VGEC.

Techno Aspire, the name itself signifies the spirit of an engineer-passion, ambition and dedication to explore unseen corners and surmount immeasurable heights.

This annual technical event gives a rostrum to aspirants from all over Gujarat to project their proficiency and intellect, with an aim to abridge the gap between theoretical concepts learned and the fascinating practical implementation that would follow.

For 7 years, we have been conducting this event with companionship, control and creativity, making it grand year after year, and like the apparent human tendency, our thirst is not quelled. Our aim is to create an environment that fosters the same and hopefully we are directed on the right path to realize this again on

29th and 30th March, 2012
