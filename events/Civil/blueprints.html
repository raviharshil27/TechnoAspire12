• Engineers like to solve problems.
  • If there are no problems handily available, they will create their own problems.
  • With this view in mind, the event aims at serving as a platform for all those who wish to contribute in the development of technological research.
  • This competition is aimed at bringing out innovative ideas from young and ignited minds.
  • The presentation will be judged on the basis of the originality of the content including the case-studies and its organization, effectiveness of delivery and adherence to allocated presentation time.
  • Each team would consist of maximum 2 members.
  • The time allotted for presentation is 10 minutes (8 min presentation + 2 min question-answer).
  • The abstract of the study (minimum 5 pages of A4 size, having title in font size 14 and body font size 12, Times New Roman) should be sent through e-mail to civil_technoaspire2011@live.in latest by the midnight of 24th February 2011. Selected entries would be intimated by e-mail.
  • Multiple entries are not allowed i.e., participant cannot participate for two times or even for two different topics.
  • Team would have to prepare a PowerPoint Presentation on a topic related to Civil Engineering/Civil Technology. Also, additional audio-video clips can be included in presentation. And on the day of event, contestants are required to bring hard copy of their abstract.
  • Topics such as Green building concept & Carbon credits, Innovation in construction techniques, Low
  • cost/Affordable housing, Advanced technology of mass transit/Intelligent Transportation system, Earthquake resistant design, Energy efficient design & construction of buildings, Vastu Science or any other topic related to Civil Engineering/Civil Technology can be included.
  • Presentation would be judged on the basis of Selection of Topic, Presentation content and Presentation skills.
  • Final decision will be authorized by the judges and shall be binding to all.
  • At any time these rules can be updated, without any prior notice.
