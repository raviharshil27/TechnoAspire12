• Town Planning has been a concept adopted from the time man has begin to live. Town Planning can be seen andobserved in age old civilizations like Mohenjo Daro and Harappa. Cities have been a part and parcel of human life for almost last two centuries.
  • This event comprises of a Model Representation of section of a city. The Plan of the city has to pre prepared. This event helps to understand different ideas and concepts, as well as the model explains different criteria of town planning and basic principles of city development.
    • Materials
  • Following materials would be provided:
  • 2 general sized Thermocol sheets having thickness 35mm
  • 2 general sized Thermocol sheets having thickness 15mm
  • 1 general sized Cardboard
  • Fevicol
  • Pins
  • Straws
  • Colour papers (Colouring/Painting materials other than colour papers can be brought by participants)
    • Specifications
  • The section of city should at least contain following:
  • 2 Residential complexes
  • 1 Skyscraper
  • 1 Bridge (Over river or Flyover)
  • City should be developed on a riverside or hillside
  • 1 Public building (Such as Bank, College, Police Station, Fire Station, Water tank etc.)
  • Roads should be clearly visible and as per bylaws of Town planning
  • Innovative ideas are acceptable and should be suitable for the city.
    • Judging Criteria
  • Concepts of Town planning used.
  • For residential zone Setbacks, Light plane and Margins will be considered.
  • Environmental friendliness of city.
  • Maximum criteria covered.
  • Minimum material wasted.
  • Maximum amount of work completed in 3 hours.
  • Good aesthetic view and elevation of city.
  • Dimensions of the roads.
    1. Each team would consist of maximum 3 participants and the time provided to complete the task is 3 hrs.
    2. Tools required for cutting are to be brought by participants.
    3. No extra material would be allowed other than provided at event spot.
    4. No excess material would be provided other than specified.
    5. Each team will have to construct according to dimensions provided in specification chart and also any type of damage done to property may lead to disqualification of the team.
    6. Final decision will be authorized by event judges and shall be binding to all.
    7. At any time the rules can be updated, without any prior notice.
