• Auto Cad has been a concept for the fast generation, which takes minimum time to complete our work and draw the plan of building or machinery very fast and accurately. It is highly adopted in the fast moving competitive world. The competition aims to improve the accuracy and drawing skill of the participants.
    • JUDGING CRITERIA:
  • The total marks obtained by the team / participant in both rounds will be considered for the final result.
    Round 1:
  • MCQ The-1st round consist of 20 MCQ.
    Round 2:
    Drawing Creation
    Following are the parameters on which the drawing will be judged.
  • Drawing creation
  • Internal & external dimensions
  • Other details (Text, Doors, Windows etc..)
  • Completion of drawing
  • Judge points
  • Each team would consist of maximum of 2 members and Participant may also take part in the event individually.
  • Students from different educational institute may also form a team.
  • The event will be conducted in 2 Rounds.
  • The 1st Round consists of 20 MCQ and the time period for the 1st round will be 20 Minutes.
  • The 2nd Round consists of drawing creation. The Drawing will be provided on the spot and it would be common to all. The time duration for the 2nd Round will be 1 hour.
  • The drawing must be according to the dimensions provided in the given drawing. Any additional features can be added to the drawing.
  • Any Damage done to the college property may lead to the disqualification of the team.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding to all.
  • The organizer should reserve all the rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit.
