• In this event, three rounds related to circuit solution, rectification and designing will be there.
    • Round 1:
  • All the participants will have to rectify one faulty circuit which will be provided by us.
    • Round 2:
  • All the selected participants from round one will be provided by one circuit, they have to solve this circuit & submit the truth table of it.
  • The top three participants of this round will be qualified for next round.
    • Round 3:
  • This is the final round and the participants will have to design one circuit and describe it.
  • The winner of this round will be decided by expert judges.
