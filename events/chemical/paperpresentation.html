RULES AND REGULATIONS:-
  • The Contest is a team event consisting of maximum 2 participants.
  • The teams are supposed to send the abstracts before deadline to be eligible for the event.
  • Based on the abstract, selection of the teams for the finals will be held and the selected
    participants will be informed about the same.
  • The Report as well as the abstract should be mandatorily in the IEEE format.
  • All papers must be sent electronically to the mail id chemtrix@vgecg.ac.in
  • Details of both the team members: names, department, College Details, email ID and Contact Number should be sent along with the paper.
  • The main event will be an on-stage event.
  • The teams shortlisted on the basis of the abstract will be required to present the paper.
  • The total time that will be provided for the presentation is 10 minutes (7 minutes for presentation and 3 minutes for question and answer).
  • The decision of the judges would be final and binding.
