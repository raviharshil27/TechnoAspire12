    • 1. The solution proposed by the team
    • 2. Loopholes/drawbacks of the solution proposed

  • Participants have to prepare the poster based on the guidelines mentioned below before the event.
    • The Contest is a team event consisting of maximum 2 participants.
    • On or before the last day of submission of abstract, the participants are supposed to send in the brief overview of their poster.
  • The abstract must contain:
    • 1. Description of the poster
    • 2. Description of the idea/solution
    • 3. Potential benefits of the solution
    • 4. The team is supposed to register themselves on or before the last date of submission of the abstract.
  • Based on the abstract, selection of the teams for the finals will be held and the selected participants will be informed about the same.
  • Details of both the team members: names, department, College Details, email ID and Contact Number should be sent along with the paper.
  • The poster is to be made on an A2 size paper.
  • Any modifications will not be permitted on the poster once submitted for presentation.
  • The decision of the judges would be final and binding.

    Disqualifications:Plagiarism and any other act or means if found improper or not abiding to the rules will be disqualified directly.
