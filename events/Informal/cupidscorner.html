The event attracts young aspirants to spread their charm on the opposite sex. Not only it ends here. This gives platform to show their hidden talent, mesmerize the other gender. The best pair becomes the heartthrob of the event.
This would contain three rounds to bring up the most deserving couple.
  • Round 1: It would have the written questions by which participants’ personal likes/dislikes, strength/weakness, emotional and romance quotient can be determined. Accordingly, pairs will be made.
  • Round 2: This round will consist of jobs which will need coordination in the pairs to qualify for the next round.
  • Round 3: In this round, the pair will be given a time of 10 mins to know each other. After that,questions will be asked to them about each other. The pair which participants know each other well, wins the CUPID’S CORNER.
