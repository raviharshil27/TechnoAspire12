SCRATCH YOUR HEAD


Description :
  • General Knowledge always revolves around current affairs, movies, sports, drama, nations, panorama –an ecstatic philosophy that enriches the world.
  • So if you are a master blaster or ‘Bombast-Consumate’ performer then it’s a challenge for you to participate in this ‘Scratch Your Head’.

Round – 1 [ELIMINATON ROUND]
  • Elimination is the biggest trouble to any Manager to handle. So, the elimination is paper based test
  • Questions will be displayed on the projector screen and the answers are to be written on the paper provided.
  • Audio/Visual Questions can also be asked.
  • Questions from nursery level to present day, rhymes to poetry, social studies, mathematics, science, literature, philosophy, arts, music, geography, history, management, awards, sports, politics, current affairs, nations, wars etc. will make up the question set.But yes, technical background will also be a part.
  • No negative marking and 30 questions.
  • No MCQs so it’s a fair chance for a deserving person to qualify.
Round – 2 [MEGA CONNECT]
  • There will be 10 teams qualifying for this round.
  • 2 sets of 6 Questions will be asked, the answers to individual questions will relate you to another specific answer for which BONUS POINTS will be awarded.
  • Marking Scheme:
     10 points for the answer and +50/-25 for the connect.
     10 points for the answer and +40/-20 for the connect.
     10 points for the answer and +30/-15 for the connect.
     10 points for the answer and +20/-10 for the connect.
     10 points for the answer and +10/-05 for the connect.
     10 points for the answer and +05/-00 for the connect.
Round – 3 [GRAND FINALE]
  • 5 teams will be qualifying for the finale.
  • This round will include the following rounds:
    • Indefinite Bounce
    • Rapid Fire [10 questions per team within 90 sec.]
    • Leg Pulling
    • Audio/Visual Round


Rules
  • There will be 3 rounds.
  • Cross-institutional participation is allowed.
  • 2 or 3 persons per team
  • Under terms of any dispute decision of Event Manager will be final.
