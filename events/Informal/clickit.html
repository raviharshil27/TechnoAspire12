Are you ready to Snappit!?

TechnoAspire brings you unique opportunity to showcase your talents with camera !
  • ROUND 1 : All the registered participant have to submit their best clicked minimum 10 photos(SOFTCOPY) on the first day of event to the event manager.
    From the submitted photographs, the participants will be judged n shortlisted for the second round.
    The result of first round will be displayed on event desk after a gap of an hour.
  • ROUND 2: For the second round, the shortlisted contestants have to click the photographs within the VGEC campus area.
    They have to submit their minimum 15 best clicked photos to the event manager/volunteer.
    Each participant will be given time of 2 hours for the live photography along the campus.
    From these above submitted clicks, the best will b declared as winner.


    RULES :
    Participants have to bring their own camera along with the USB cable/Card reader for transfer of photos for judging.
