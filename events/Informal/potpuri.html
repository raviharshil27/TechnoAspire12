POTPOURRI

  • As the name suggests, a Potpourri (originally Fr.) is a hodgepodge or in desi-speak a “khichdi” of puzzles.
  • Likely inclusions are Pictionary, Ditloid, Rebus, Mensa puzzles and (for Dan Brown fans) Anagrams too.
  • Kindly ignore the mind mumbling jargon coz we here at Potpourri are committed to entertain you completely (and also awarding you with prizes in the process)
  • Pre-requisites : A pen and numerous Grey Cells.

    Round 1 :

    • Some mind boggling questions would be given including Pictionary.
    • Particiants would be required to think out of the box solve the questions.

    Round 2 :
    • The format of the rounds would be declared on the day itself.
    • Participants will be presented with a variety of challenging Word Games and puzzles.

Rules:
  • Team Size – 2 or 3
  • In case of any disputes, the final authority rests with the event co-ordinator.
