This is non-technical event based on participant's skill, strength and accuracy. This event is arranged for refreshments and fun for the visitors. This event is useful for getting more participants in other events.
General rules:
  • There are 4 levels and 1 super level if needed.
  • Super level is a surprise level.
  • Each levels of this event are strictly of 1 minute.
  • The participants will pass through 4 levels, they only can play super level.
  • There is no any type of wild card entry.
  • Event managers and the persons who are related to event will not take part in this event.
  • every participant will take part only 1 time.
  • final decision should be taken by the event judge.
  • winners are the participants who will stand till the end.
  • there are 4 levels of event named as under.
  1. Defying gravity  In this level , participant has to keep 3 balloons in air by his/her hands.  The participant who can't fulfill this condition has to eliminate from the competition.
  2. This blows  In this level , first of all participant should fill air in the balloon.  Then he/she has to make fall down the glasses on the ground placed on the table.  Each participants have to be fallen down 15 glasses in one minute.
  3. Ping pong ball  9 glasses , filled with water will be put on one table at some distance.  The participants have to stand away from the table from some distance.  The participants should be provided a bucket full of TT ball.  The TT ball should be bounced on an intermediate table.  At least 5 balls should fall in a glass out of nine glasses.
  4. Noodling around  Each participant will be given one noodle stick . and stick should be put in the mouth.  In this level , one should not use their hands.  Each participant should manage to keep at least five fryums in the sticks.
