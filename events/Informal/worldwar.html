WAR OF THE WORLDS


DESCRIPTION:-
  • Entire world is now in trouble,because a war is going to happen.
  • You are the representative of your country and you are present in the war room.
  • You have to act smart, play wise and fight passionately for the survival of your country with the limited amount of resources available in your country.
  • “Think With Your Heart; But Play With Your Mind.“

Elimination Round
  • For eliminations,Group Discussions ( G.D. ) will be held.
  • The topics of G.D. will contain subjects affecting country security, war and current affairs.
  • Nine participants will be selected for the finals.

Final Round
  • Each participant will represent the country allotted to them.
  • Participants will have to make war and treaty according to given enmity level and initial resources.
  • The event will flow with the wars and treaty.
  • The country which will win the war will get some amount of initial resources.
  • At the end of the game, the country which will have the highest amount of resources remaining with themselves will be declared as the winner.
