CRIME TIME

DESCRIPTION:-
  • The Scotland Yard is under fire, drawing huge amounts of criticism due to its inefficiency and particularly due to quick case closures by Consultant Sherlock Holmes.
  • Fresh reports of a homicide in sub urban London have surfaced and as Inspector Lestrade the entire Yard is counting on you for its revival.
  • Problem Statement: Participants will be given an account of a fictional crime scene and they are expected to solve the crime by coming up with a logical explanation.
  • Pre Requisites:Razor Sharp Observation, Imagination and an uncanny ability to connect the dots (either forward or backward)
Rules:-
  • Team Size : Max. 3
  • A written account of crime, suspect profiles and transcripts of interrogation will be provided.
  • The participants will be given 2 hours to solve the crime
  • The participants are expected to submit a logical explanation of the chain of events that led to the crime as well as mantion the clues which helped them to reach their conclusion.
  • In case any dispute, the final authority rests with the Event manager and Faculty Co-ordinator.
