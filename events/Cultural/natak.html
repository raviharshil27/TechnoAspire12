DESCRIPTION:-
'The Street Play' rolls back the year's usage of open space,fundamental right of speech and showcasing
an array of acts with socially relevant messages from those "olden days".
Authentic plays aiming at the common man passing by on the street are displayed with three Es: Excitement, Energy and Enthusiasm
A tinge of imagination, shade of reality and desire to express social awareness is what NUKKAD-NATAK is all about.
It is more powerful than any other mode of theater because of its proximity between the audience and actors.
RULES AND REGULATIONS:-
  • Team size should not exceed 18.
  • Musical instruments are allowed. (Innovative musical instruments like cans, bottles, etc. may fetch you some brownie points).
  • No electronic instruments are allowed.
  • Please note that the medium of communication may be English, Hindi or Bi-lingual.
  • Clothing or actions that could be perceived as obscene, lewd, vulgar or plainly offensive is strictly prohibited.
  • It should be a one act play, which implies, once the actors start performing, they don't get off the arena before the completion of the play.
  • Judges' and coordinators' decision will be final and will not be subjected to any change.
