Robo tressure is an event in which the team has to find the treasure with the help of the robots solving some clues and also covering some track with ups and downs.
    ROUND 1 :
  • In the first round four team have to start all together. Which ever team reaches to the treasure in less time and maximum points wins that round. To reach to the treasure there will be 6-7 different ways.
  • The difficulty level of each path is different. Depending upon the path you choose you will be awarded points.
  • On the way there will be some clues and some questions the player can move ahead if he solves it correctly.
    • ROUND 2 :
  • The top 20 teams of the first round again compete with each other. The difficulty level increases. And there will be more questions to solve. And the track also becomes more challenging.
    • FINAL ROUND :
  • The top 5 team find the real treasure and win the event.
    • CRITERIA :
  • A team of 2-3 persons with their robot.
