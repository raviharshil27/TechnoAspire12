About

Your ROBO WAR shifts to the next level as we bring you “ ROBO WAR: TECH8 “..War TO Win. . . .

Objectives

Design and construct a robot capable of fighting a two-way and three- way tournament inside the arena.

Team Specification

A team may consist of a maximum of 4 members. Students from different educational institutes admitted in undergraduate program can form a team.

  1. The maximum duration will be 5 minutes for 1st round and there is 6 minutes from 2nd round onwards.
  2. Any team that is not ready at the time specified will be disqualified from the competition automatically.
  3. The machine would be checked for its safety before the competition and would be discarded if found unsafe for other participants and spectators.
  4. The name of your machine must be prominently displayed on the machine.
  5. The organizers reserve the rights to change any or all of the above rules as they see fit. Change in rules, if any will be highlighted on the website and notified to the registered teams.
  6. Violation of any the above rules will lead to disqualification.
  7. Judges' decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.

    CONSTRAINTS
    Dimensions and Weight
  • The robot should not be more than 40cm*40cm*40cm in size at any time during game-play.
  • The weight of the any robot should not in any case exceed 7 kg.
  • And battery limit is 36volt.
MOBILITY
  • All robots must have easily visible and controlled mobility in order to compete.
  • Flying and climbing over the cage is not allowed.
