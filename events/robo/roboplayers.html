This is an event of Robotics in which two robots start their running in path parallely to get something (means a piece of wood) which is assumed as a piece of gold & the robot who will get this piece of gold first & complete the path with minimum penalty ,then that team is the winner. So guys gear up your robo to participate in PLAYERS 2012.
  1. maximum 4 participants per team.
  2. Dimension of robo should not exceed:-(30cm*30cm*30cm).
  3. The robot can be wired or wireless.
  4. An individual or team cannot enter multiple identical robots in the same event.
  5. Toss will be for choosing the side of path.
  6. Any damage on the arena created by the robots or team members will lead to participant’s disqualification.
  7. Under the term of any dispute the decision of the event manager will be final.
  8. The organizers reserve the right to change any of the rules, if they consider necessary.
  9. The changes will be notified.
