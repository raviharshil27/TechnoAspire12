Match Format:
  • The group of 4 participants will be in the one match..first two participants will enter in the next round of the game.the 1st round will be sprint track...
  • In quarters, the game mode will be circuit..
  • In semis the game will be more tough and game mode will be drag.
  • In finals, game mode will be best of 3 races (Sprint, Circuit (2 laps),knockout)
  • Players can use any car available in the game.
  • No match will be played for the third spot.

  • Players must be present in the tournament area at the notifications schedule. Players are advised to be present a few minutes before the schedule to avoid any unpredictable delay. If the player is not present at the notification schedule, he shall be disqualified.
  • Players will be given 5 minutes to choose their car and tune them before racing.
  • The players have the freedom to bring and use their own controlling device, that is, mouse, keyboard, headphones etc.
  • On any type of disconnections, this procedure will be followed:
  • *Upon any disconnection of the connection between match players due to system, network, PC, and/or power problems/issues the match shall be restarted in case a winner for that game has not been declared..
  • * If the disconnection is deemed to be unintentional by the referee, then the match will be restarted in case the winner of that race has not been decided. If any player does not agree to a match restart then that player will be disqualified.
  • Use of cheat codes/programs prohibited..
  • Upon discovery of any player committing any violations regarded as unfair play, that player will be disqualified from the tournament.
  • During the course of any match, the operations staff and/or referee may determine other actions to be unfair play at any time.
  • All the above rules should be followed without argument with the organizers, the violation of which may result in player or team disqualification, the decision to do the same lies in the hand of the organizers present there.
